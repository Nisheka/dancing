Rules

Prathama General Rules

Examination of Prathama – After study for two years.


A Candidate shall be required to Pass the Theory & Practical Examination Separately.

Traditional Rules.

To Pass the examination, a Candidate needs to secure 33% or above of Total Marks in Theory and Practical Separately.

Formal Rules

In case of discrepancy between Marksheet & Mark Register of the Bhathkhande Sangit Vidyapith Lucknow, the marks entered in the marks register shall beated final


If any discrepancy is found in the Marksheet / Result, same should be reported immediately to the Registrar, Bhathkhande Sangit Vidyapith, Lucknow in written, duly mentioning the discrepancy along with Photo copy of the Marksheet.
