Dance drama- Sri Lankan Classical Dance

History

The Kolam dance-drama is a unique form of artistic expression in the low country regions of Sri Lanka. Performers adorn masks representing various figures, including animals, kings, and dignitaries. Unlike traditional rituals, Kolam focuses on amusement and social commentary, offering a blend of entertainment and satire.
