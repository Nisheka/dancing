why choose us

A web-based application for dancers to promote dance and provide resources and opportunities for people to engage with the dance community. Some features of this website include a dance events calendar, a directory of the nearest dance choreographers, Artist profiles and dance videos and workshops. In addition to that using a chatbot system that makes the system not dull for every dance user to use to guide their "Visharad" exam and direction of university studies.
